about us

DIAS UNITED SPORTS CLUB is a Registered Organization under the Society Registration Act 1860 of Government of India. Hosted the official F5WC World Finals Qualifiers Tournament in Western Region of India. It’s an Amateur World Cup of 5-A-Side. Official kit Sponsor for Under-19 Indian Football Team which participated in Asian School Games at South Korea 2015-2016. Organized official District & Divisional level Competition for DSO (District Sports Organization) Government of India. Organizes Official District, State & National level Tournaments & Championship of recognized Association & National Federation. Participates at District, State, National & International Competition. Provided players from District to International level. Provide free Coaching facilities to under Privileged Children. Performing ART classes for kids Development.

10 MAJOR POINTS TO GIVE SPORTS A NEW FACE IN INDIA

better infrastructure

There is a need to improve & renovate existing Sports facilities in terms of spaces in school & government owned Playgrounds. There has to be a plan to create new spaces across the Length & Breadth of the COUNTRY.

Educate Coaches & Its Utilization

Education is not only important for Student/Players but it’s also important for Coaches to be educated so that they can impart their KNOWLEDGE to Students/Players. Utilizing knowledge of both Player & Coach plays a vital role.

Early Start Up & Stop Age Fraud

To know the correct age to start the playing sport is also important. For example: FOOTBALL, start age is 6 years. But special programs to be designed or 3 to 5 years old kids, so Fitness activities can be start from 3 years.

Age fraud has to be stopped. As early the child enrolls, we need to have Centralized Registration System (CRS). So records are maintained right from the age player enters the sports.

Exposure National Level Competition

It’s very much important that Players & Sports reach all partsof theCountry. So participating in Exposure Tournaments is also very much Important.

Role Model

Like few great sports personalities like Sachin Tendulkar, Leander Paes, Major Dhyanchand, Milkha Singh.We need to create players from India to be role model of tomorrow.

Governance

Need a Professional Administration for governing the sport at various levels & also assisting various sports programs initiativeprofessionally.

Sports As A Carrier

Sport is not just a carrier for players. “Awareness for Parents & Children forchoosing Carrier Path.” Various aspects involved for buildup in sports carrier

FOUNDATION of DUSC

DIAS UNITED SPORTS CLUB was founded in 2009 by Late Mr. Jacinto Andrew Fernandes who was a legendary football player and renowned in INDIA for his lifetime dedication for the game at Maharashtra & several International events and some of the greatest tournaments like ROVERS CUP, D.C.M CUP, DURAND CUP & FEDERATION CUP under the guidance of his father. Mrs. Elizabeth also played at district and national level through "WOMENS FOOTBALL FEDEREATION OF INDIA". DIAS UNITED SPORTS CLUB is dualy registered with the CHARITY COMMISIONER & the SOCEITY REGISTRATION ACT 1860 under the Government of INDIA.
